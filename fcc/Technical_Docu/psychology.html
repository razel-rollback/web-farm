The study of human behavior

Introduction

The study of human behavior is a fascinating and complex field dedicated to understanding why individuals think, feel, and act the way they do. It draws upon a wide array of disciplines, offering insights into the intricate mechanisms that drive human actions and interactions.

Example of a simple behavioral pattern in pseudocode:

if (stimulus == positive) { response = "approach"; } else { response = "avoid"; }
Core Disciplines

Key disciplines include:

  • Psychology
  • Sociology
  • Anthropology
  • Neuroscience
  • Economics

decision = evaluate(needs, environment, options);

Important aspects of human behavior study:

  4. Cognitive processes
  5. Emotional regulation
